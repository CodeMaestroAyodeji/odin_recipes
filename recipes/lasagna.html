Lasagna Recipe

A classic Italian dish consisting of layers of lasagna noodles, savory meat sauce, creamy ricotta cheese, and melted mozzarella cheese, baked to perfection.

Ingredients

  • Lasagn noodles
  • Ground beef or Italian sausage
  • Ricotta cheese

Steps

  1. Cook the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions until al dente.
  2. Brown the ground beef or Italian sausage in a skillet over medium heat.
  3. Layer the cooked noodles, meat sauce, and ricotta cheese in a baking dish, and repeat until all ingredients are used up. Top with mozzarella cheese.
