Chocolate Chip Cookies Recipe

Homemade cookies bursting with gooey chocolate chips, crispy on the outside and chewy on the inside, perfect for satisfying your sweet tooth cravings.

Ingredients

  • All-purpose flour
  • Butter
  • Chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. In a mixing bowl, cream together the butter and sugar until light and fluffy. Mix in the flour until well combined, then fold in the chocolate chips.
  3. Drop spoonfuls of dough onto the prepared baking sheet and bake for 10-12 minutes, or until the edges are golden brown. Allow to cool before serving.
