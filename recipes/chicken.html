Chicken Alfredo Recipe

A delicious pasta dish featuring tender grilled or pan-seared chicken breast served over al dente fettuccine noodles, smothered in rich and creamy Alfredo sauce.

Ingredients

  • Fettuccine pasta
  • Chicken breast
  • Alfredo sauce

Steps

  1. Cook the fettuccine pasta according to the package instructions until al dente.
  2. Grill or pan-sear the chicken breast until fully cooked.
  3. In a saucepan, heat the Alfredo sauce until warm. Serve the cooked pasta topped with sliced chicken breast and Alfredo sauce.
